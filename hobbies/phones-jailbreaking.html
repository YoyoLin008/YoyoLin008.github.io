Tweaks in action

Written highlights of the mods and experiments that keep these phones interesting.

Home screen tinkering is the fastest way to refresh a device. Switching icon packs, widgets, and gestures can make an old phone feel brand new, and I keep notes on which launchers balance speed with customization.

Performance tweaks are a careful game. Kernel changes and battery governors can add hours of life or break stability, so I record each experiment with benchmarks and day-to-day impressions before deciding if it sticks.

I still love keeping a retro favorite around. Older devices with clever hardware features or quirky UX remind me why modding matters: you get to keep the parts that made a phone special while updating the bits that aged less gracefully.
